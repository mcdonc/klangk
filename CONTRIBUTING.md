# Contributing to klangk

Thanks for your interest in contributing. Please read this before opening an
issue or pull request — it explains how submissions are handled.

## Default-closed policy

To keep the project reviewable as the volume of AI-generated ("slop")
submissions rises, **newly opened issues and pull requests will be closed
automatically** (once the corresponding automation is live). This is not a
judgment on your contribution — it's a filter that shifts the cost of sifting
low-signal submissions off the maintainer, so genuine contributions don't get
buried.

### How to get your issue or PR reconsidered

If your contribution is substantive, get it back in front of a maintainer by
doing **one** of the following:

1. **Reply in the thread** with context — what you're trying to do, what you
   tried, why it matters, and (for bugs) how to reproduce it. A single
   substantive comment is enough.
2. **Email** [chrism@plope.com](mailto:chrism@plope.com) with a link to the
   issue/PR and the same context.

A maintainer (often assisted by an LLM-driven review tool — see _Admin review_
below) will then reassess and reopen promising items.

### What counts as "human-sounding"

A reopen is not earned by clicking the reopen button or posting a one-character
reply. We look for **substantive engagement** — specifics about your situation,
evidence, or a clear description of the problem and what you've tried. This
filter exists to separate real contributors from automated noise; it is not
meant to deter humans, and a real sentence or two clears it without trouble.

### Trusted contributors

Known/trusted contributors (maintained in `.github/CODEOWNERS`) will bypass
auto-close so routine, reviewed work isn't frictioned. If you've contributed
before and would like to be added, mention it in your reply or email.

## Admin review

Reconsidered items are triaged with the help of an LLM-assisted review process:
each reopened or recently-opened item is scored for substance and grounding,
and borderline items get a deeper review before a maintainer decides to act or
re-close. This keeps maintainer time focused on contributions that have earned
it.

## The short version

Write a real sentence or two about your issue or PR, post it (or email it), and
a human will take a look. That's it.
